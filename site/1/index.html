How it works

Stage → scan → decide

Nothing installs until you approve. Data stays on disk unless you opt into Socket.

  1. 1

    Stage

    Package to .scguard/cache.

  2. 2

    Scan

    Heuristics + optional Socket score.

  3. 3

    Decide

    Approve or stop the install.
